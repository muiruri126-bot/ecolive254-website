Let's Join Forces to Restore the Mangroves

December 6, 2023 Updated March 13, 2024 Ecolive Kenya
Back to News

Mangroves are the frontline of our Source-to-Sea strategy — shielding our coastline, storing carbon, and providing nurseries for marine life.

Through community-led planting, we are bringing these vital coastal forests back to life. Each seedling restored strengthens the natural barrier that protects shorelines from erosion and supports the fisheries that coastal families depend on.

Restoring mangroves is a shared responsibility — and a shared opportunity. Join us as we restore the mangroves, one seedling at a time.
